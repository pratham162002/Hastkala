ABOUT US

We are a brand that celebrates sustainability, and our designs reflect the style of modern Indian homeowners. Through our collection, we try to preserve and embrace age-old crafts, weaving tales of heritage in our products and fostering a community that shares and supports the journey of local artisans.

HOW WE WERE BORN?

Rooted in the cultural mosaic of Rajasthan, our founders, with a heritage in exporting handicrafts, envisioned our brand as a celebration of India's artistry. Beyond North India’s borders, our brand serves as a platform spotlighting undiscovered design brilliance.
